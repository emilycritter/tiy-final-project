# where.*art*.thou

https://where-art-thou.herokuapp.com

where.art.thou isn't just your typical online marketplace for buying and selling artwork, because where.art.thou is all about the WHERE. The application offers users the unique experience of purchasing art and supporting artists in their local community. In addition to viewing art by location, user's can filter art by preference (price, category, etc.), save their favorite pieces, contact artists, and purchase artwork with payment processing via Stripe.
